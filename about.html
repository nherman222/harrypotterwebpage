About Me

Hi there! My name is Nina Herman and I'm a sophomore at Dartmouth College pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Economics.

I've moved four times but all to houses within 30 minutes of each other, just outside Phildelphia (it's a long story). I love to play squash and tennis, do yoga, and hike. (And, clearly, I love Harry Potter. But who doesn't?)

As for developing, VS code is my go-to. My favorite part of developing is the planning. I love talking through how I plan to implement an idea (whether that's talking to myself or others). I love scribbling on a piece of paper for hours until I finally understand how I'm going to bring what was previously just an idea to life.

With DALI, I’m most excited to think through how to implement ideas with other people who share my excitement for developing. Additionally, I hope to use my skills to develop applications and technologies that have a measurable impact on people’s lives and lifestyles. Not only am I excited to apply and hone my current skills, but I enjoy learning new ones, too. I had a great time developing this webpage by learning through YouTube, and I hope to continue to build upon my skills by applying them in an environment like DALI.
